Spin

In the Battle Café you can spin around with Milcery to evolve it into Alcremie.

Alcremie has several forms, the Alcremie's form you get will depend on the sweet you use, the day time, how many spins you do and the spin method (clockwise/counter-clockwise).

You do 1 spin per second. You can't do more than 20 spins.

There are nine Alcremie's forms to unlock per sweet, you can get them by spinning around with the following conditions:

Spin chances

You'll get 3 daily spin chances, then 1 spin chance will recharge every two hours. If you get all Alcremie's forms, the spin chances will recharge every hour.

You can store up to 3 spins chances, the max. amount of stored spin chances increases if you get all Alcremie's forms from a sweet (up to 7).

Sweets

Berries are needed to make a sweet. You can't spin if you don't have enough berries.

If you get all Alcremie's forms as shiny, the berry price will be halved.

Day time

Milcery has a wrong sense of the time, when evolving, it considers dusk time between 7pm - 7:59pm
